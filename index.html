A note from the founder
"After 10 years in HR, I kept seeing the same problems repeat themselves — companies overpaying for average recruitment, employees unaware of their own statutory rights, and professionals struggling to find a trusted CA. I built PeopleFirst HRs to solve all three, in one place, with complete transparency on fees and zero fluff."

Over a decade spent across recruitment, HR operations, and people consulting — working with startups, mid-sized companies, and large enterprises — gave me an inside view of how broken the hiring and tax advisory ecosystem really is for most businesses and individuals in India.

On the recruitment side, small and mid-sized companies routinely pay inflated fees to agencies that offer no transparency, no guarantees, and no accountability. On the other side, salaried employees and small business owners have no easy way to find a trustworthy CA for something as routine as filing an ITR or understanding which tax regime saves them money — they rely on word of mouth or luck.

PeopleFirst HRs was built to bridge that gap. Free tools that give people accurate answers instantly. A CA marketplace where professionals can find verified, reviewed experts with clear fees. And a recruitment service with a simple, honest fee — 8.5% for tech, 7.5% for non-tech — paid only when we deliver. No retainers, no surprises, no empty promises.

The problem we're solving
For employers
Recruitment agencies charge 15–20% with no guarantees and zero transparency. SMBs lose lakhs on bad hires with no recourse.
For individuals
Most salaried employees don't know their gratuity, correct tax regime, or statutory rights. There's no simple, trusted place to check.
For CA professionals
Most CAs have no digital presence. Clients can't discover them, compare fees, or read reviews — the matchmaking is purely offline and unreliable.
